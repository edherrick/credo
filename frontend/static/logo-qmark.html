My recommendation
C — The Wax Seal, refined
The question mark in white stroke asks the question cleanly. The oversized indigo dot below is the answer — sealed, certified, on record. It's the two-act story of Credo in a single mark:

Ask the question. Commit to the answer. The answer doesn't change.

The cross inside the seal can be dropped for the nav (too small) and brought back for larger contexts like a favicon or hero. The two-tone white/indigo also gives you flexibility — white question on any dark surface, indigo dot always the accent.
